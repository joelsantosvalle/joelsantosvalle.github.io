Research

My research interests are centered around multimodal perception for intelligent systems. I am especially interested in how models can combine visual, audio, and sensor-based information to remain reliable under real-world uncertainty.

Current topics I am interested in include audio-visual scene understanding, robust token compression, low-light perception, embodied AI, and perception for robotics.
